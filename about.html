Megi Brahimaj, DMD

Dr. Megi Brahimaj is a graduate from Stony Brook University’s School of Dental Medicine where she was recognized with the “Excellence in Esthetic Dentistry” by the Academy of Esthetic Dentistry, “Excellence in General Dentistry” by the Academy of General Dentistry. She continued her two-year post-doctoral training at the reputable James J. Peters VA Medical Center where she treated US veterans with medically-compromised conditions and complex dental needs. There, she gained extensive experience managing patients with dental phobias and complicated oral conditions. She became proficient in prosthodontics, endodontics, oral surgery and implantology.

Dr. Brahimaj prides herself upon the relationships she builds with her patients and the individualized comprehensive dental care she provides. She finds importance in communication and upholding a meticulous and detail-oriented standard of care. She is passionate about treating her patients and providing excellent dentistry in a warm, welcoming, and comfortable environment.

Dr. Brahimaj is a member of several professional organizations including the American Dental Association, American Academy of General Dentistry, American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry, Albanian Medical Society and Spear Education. Outside of the office, Dr. Brahimaj enjoys spending time with her family and friends, traveling, reading, volleyball and giving back to the community as evidenced by her involvement in local and international outreach.
